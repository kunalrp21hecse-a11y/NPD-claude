Opportunity Score = F(0.40) + S(0.35) + V(0.25)
F = frequency_count / max_frequency ← normalised 0–1
S = (6 − avg_rating) / 5 ← severity, inverted rating scale
V = avg_helpful_votes / max_helpful_votes ← social validation
Final score mapped 50–100 using power normalisation (exponent 0.6) to create meaningful spread between ranks.

Analyst's Note — Early Signals
Western Trends Heading to India
Beyond what the data shows — consumer behaviours from the West that typically arrive in India 2–3 years later. Not data-backed, but market-aware.
🧬
Skin Microbiome Care
Probiotic-based skincare is growing rapidly in the US and EU — targeting the skin's bacterial ecosystem, not just surface symptoms. Indian consumers are beginning to encounter this language.
Early Signal — US/EU
🧪
Clinical-Grade Personalisation
Brands like Curology and Function of Beauty have normalised quiz-based formulation in the West. Our data confirms personalisation_demand is already top-5 in India — the gap is validated.
Data-Confirmed Crossover
♻️
Refill & Zero-Waste Models
Sustainable packaging is a growing pain point in our review data. In the West, refillable deodorants and serum pods are mainstream. India's urban consumer is ready — the infrastructure isn't yet.
