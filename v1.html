Opportunity Score Formula
Score = F(0.4) + S(0.35) + V(0.25)
Frequency (40%), Severity (35%), Validation (25%) — weighted to match how Mosaic defines "unmet need value."
Severity Definition
Severity = (6 − avg_rating) / 5
Lower ratings = higher pain = higher score. A 1-star review carries 5× more weight than a 5-star complaint.
Validation Definition
Validation = avg_helpful_votes / max_votes
Helpful votes confirm the complaint resonates with a broader audience, not just one frustrated customer.

Analyst's Note — Early Signals
Western Trends Heading to India
Beyond what the data shows — emerging consumer behaviours in the West that typically arrive in India 2–3 years later. Not data-backed, but market-aware.
🧬
Skin Microbiome Care
Probiotic-based skincare is exploding in the US and EU. Products targeting the skin's bacterial ecosystem, not just surface symptoms. Indian consumers are just beginning to encounter this language.
Early Signal — US/EU
🧪
Clinical-Grade Personalisation
Brands like Curology and Function of Beauty have normalised quiz-based formulation in the West. The data shows personalisation_demand is a top complaint in India — this gap is already validated.
Data-Confirmed Crossover
♻️
Refill & Zero-Waste Models
Sustainable packaging is a growing pain point in the review data. In the West, refillable deodorants and serum pods are mainstream. India's urban consumer is ready — the infrastructure isn't yet.
