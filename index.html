How We Ranked Every Problem
We didn't just count complaints. We scored each unmet need on three dimensions — because not all complaints are equal.
40%
How Often It's Mentioned
A problem that appears in 600 reviews matters more than one in 50. Frequency tells us how widespread the pain is across customers.
611 reviews mention ingredient transparency
35%
How Angry Customers Are
Complaints from 1-star reviews hurt more than those from 4-star reviews. We weight the score higher when the frustration is deep, not mild.
Avg 2.1★ = extreme frustration signal
25%
How Many People Agree

Analyst's Note — Early Signals
Western Trends Heading to India
Beyond what the data shows — emerging consumer behaviours in the West that typically arrive in India 2–3 years later. Not data-backed, but market-aware.
🧬
Skin Microbiome Care
Probiotic-based skincare is growing rapidly in the US and EU — products targeting the skin's bacterial ecosystem, not just surface symptoms. Indian consumers are beginning to encounter this language.
Early Signal — US/EU
🧪
Clinical-Grade Personalisation
Brands like Curology and Function of Beauty have normalised quiz-based formulation in the West. Our data confirms personalisation_demand is already a top-5 complaint in India — the gap is validated.
Data-Confirmed Crossover
♻️
Refill & Zero-Waste Models
Sustainable packaging is a growing pain point in our review data. In the West, refillable deodorants and serum pods are mainstream. India's urban consumer is ready — the infrastructure isn't yet.
Early Signal — UK/EU
